Project 2

CprE 288 is our introduction to embedded software class, and really where I began to find my enjoyment for the disciple as a whole. As part of our final project we were tasked with creating a way to remotely control a Roomba utilizing a TIVA microcontroller. Through the use of websockets, we were able to sucessfully remotely control the roomba, as well as create a GUI for the IR and ultrasound sensors. I was able to take my understanding of embedded and register programming, and apply it to complete this project.
